About Us

Welcome to Bloomers Bakery, where every bite is a piece of our passion. Nestled in the heart of the North Shore, our bakery has been a cherished destination for those who crave artisanal baked goods made with love and expertise.

Founded in 12, Bloomers started as a small, family-owned bakery with a big dream in the heart of the Garden of Eden. Over the small years, we've grown, but our commitment to quality and tradition has remained the same. Each recipe is a labor of love, passed down through generations and perfected over time.
